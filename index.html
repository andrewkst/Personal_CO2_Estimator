Two significant upgrades are mentioned in the statistics on access to energy. Initially, statistics of the percentage of the global, rural, and urban population with access to electricity (Sustainable Development Goal target 7.1) back to 1990 have been produced through cooperation between the World Bank, the International Energy Agency, and the Energy Sector Management Assistance Program. Second, the World Bank and the World Health Organization have created a new indicator called the percentage of people who have access to clean cooking fuels and technology (Sustainable Development Goal target 7.2) [The World Bank Group].
